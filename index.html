A message from Saul

We don't give a plant some beer, cut off its light and expect it to grow into a healthy plant.

Why do we expect something else for us? Pornography is everywhere, it's handed to boys before they know what to do with it, normalized by a culture that profits from it, and silently destroys the lives of men who don't know where to turn or even what to call what they're feeling.

What I've seen, over and over, is that it's not just the behavior that does the damage. It's the shame. The secrecy. The belief that this makes you less of a man, less of a husband, less of a father. That something is fundamentally wrong with you.

The Wholeness Project was created so it doesn't have to stay that way. There is something in the way — but it can move. You don't have to white-knuckle through this alone, and you don't have to blow up your life to get help. You just have to take one honest step.

I'm building a space for education, community and real resources where men can do exactly that. Leave your email and I'll reach out when we're ready.

— Saul

Support & Resources

Some resources, available now

You don't have to wait for us to launch. These organizations are doing serious work around pornography, sexual addiction, and the shame that surrounds it — many of them free.

Jewish Community

Guard Your Eyes

An online community of over 30,000 Jewish men navigating pornography addiction — built specifically for those who also carry the weight of religious identity and community expectation. Peer support, accountability partnerships, and recovery tools, all rooted in a shame-free approach. Free.

guardyoureyes.com Crisis & Advocacy

Amudim

A confidential Jewish crisis resource center offering professional case management, referrals, and direct support for individuals and families dealing with sexual addiction, abuse, and related mental health challenges. Operating across 66 countries, completely confidential, and free of charge.

amudim.org Find a Therapist

OKclarity

An anonymous online directory of vetted therapists, psychiatrists, and coaches who serve the Jewish community — many with experience in sexual addiction and compulsive behavior. Browse privately, no account required. Most providers offer a free first consultation.

okclarity.com Recovery Platform

Fortify

A structured online recovery program built specifically for pornography addiction — combining education, behavioral tracking, and a peer community across 155+ countries. A free version is available for adults; no therapist required to start.

joinfortify.com 12-Step Support

Sex Addicts Anonymous

A 12-step fellowship where members define their own recovery goals and find community with others who understand compulsive sexual behavior from the inside. In-person and online meetings available worldwide. No cost to attend — ever.

saa-recovery.org Certified Specialists

Find a CSAT Therapist

CSAT (Certified Sex Addiction Therapist) is the gold-standard clinical credential for treating sexual compulsivity and pornography addiction. Use IITAP's directory to find a certified specialist in South Florida or anywhere nationwide who specializes in exactly this work.
